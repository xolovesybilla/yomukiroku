The Meaning Behind

Yomu

"Yomu" means "to read" in Japanese. This represents the core of our platform - the joy of reading, discovering new worlds through pages, and sharing those experiences with others. Every book you see here has been carefully selected, thoughtfully read, and lovingly reviewed.

Kiroku

"Kiroku" means "record" or "documentation" in Japanese. This symbolizes our mission to preserve reading memories, track reading journeys, and create a personal archive of literary adventures. Each book here tells a story not just of its content, but of when and how it was read.

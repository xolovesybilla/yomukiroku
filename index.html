Why Yomu Kiroku Exists

This library is born from a lifelong love of reading shared by Caca and Azam. While Azam enjoys reading, Caca has a passion for both buying and reading books. Since childhood, their father regularly took them book shopping, nurturing their love for reading that continues to this day.

For us, reading is more than just a hobby‚Äîit's our form of refreshment and escape. Yomu Kiroku is our way of sharing this passion with others, creating a space where book lovers can discover, borrow, and connect through stories.

How to Borrow Books

Borrowing books from our collection is simple and straightforward. Follow these easy steps to get started on your reading journey with us!

  • 1

    Find Your Book

    Browse through our collection and find the book you'd love to read.

  • 2

    Check Availability

    Make sure the book is available for borrowing (not currently loaned out).

  • 3

    Fill Borrow Form

    Provide your details: name, phone number, preferred borrow date, and duration.

  • 4

    Wait for Confirmation

    Caca or Azam will contact you via WhatsApp to confirm your borrow request.

  • 5

    Pick Up & Enjoy!

    Once confirmed, arrange pickup and enjoy your reading journey!
